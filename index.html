PandaWare

Read the Paper ↗ Read the Poster ↗ Read the Documentation Log ↗

Tech Stack

RP2040 MicroPython MyoWare 2.0 PCA9685 INA219 MG996R servos tendon-driven actuation

Overview

  • Full mechanical design and fabrication across multiple 3D-printed iterations, refining geometry for fit, range of motion, and durability.
  • Custom circuit design and assembly—integrating servo control, EMG acquisition, and power sensing on a single system.
  • Custom EMG signal pipeline with adaptive normalization, enabling reliable grip detection across varying muscle fatigue and electrode placement.
  • Closed-loop current sensing via INA219 for grip force limiting, preventing damage to objects and improving safety.
